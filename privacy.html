Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 16, 2024

At RoofMaster 360, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, company name, and contact details when you register
  • Project Data: Property addresses, roof measurements, material selections, and cost estimates you create
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed securely through third-party payment processors
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our Service, features used, and session duration

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our Service
  • Process your transactions and subscriptions
  • Send you important updates and notifications
  • Improve our Service based on usage patterns
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest. Your project data is stored securely on your device and optionally synced to our servers if you enable cloud backup.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Third parties who help us operate the Service (hosting, analytics, payment processing)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of marketing communications

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can control cookie settings in your browser. The mobile app may use analytics tools to understand usage patterns.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Third-Party Links

Our Service may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through the Service.

11. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to request deletion.
